The Plot Twist: Going Beyond Linear

Now here's where our story gets really interesting. Remember how we said a neural network is just linear regression in disguise? Well, that's only true for the simplest neural networks. They have a superpower that linear regression doesn't: they can bend!

Continue

This superpower comes from something called activation functions. We'll explore activation functions in detail in a later lesson, but for now, instead of just outputting wx + b, we can wrap it in a function that adds curves to our predictions:

\[ \text{Output} = f(wx + b) \]

Why It Matters

This is the moment where neural networks break free from the limitations of linear regression. They can capture complex patterns that linear models can only dream about!
